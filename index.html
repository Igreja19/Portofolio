KeySardinha

The Front-Office is the e-commerce storefront. It allows customers to browse, filter by category, and search for games. The user manages their profile, favorites list, and shopping cart. KeySardinha - Backend

The Back-Office is the management area, controlled by RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) permissions. The Collaborator manages the catalog (CRUD for games), digital keys, and categories, and launches promotions. The Administrator has full control, managing client accounts (block/delete) and collaborator accounts.

Mobile KeySardinha

This native Android application, built with Java, provides a mobile storefront for the KeySardinha e-commerce platform. It communicates with a RESTful API using the Volley library to handle user authentication, product browsing, and a full shopping cart and checkout process. A key feature is the offline-capable favorites list, which uses SQLite to ensure users can access their saved items even without an internet connection.

EmergencySTS (Developing)

The Frontend is the public-facing web portal for patients and visitors. Its main function is pre-triage, allowing patients to fill out a clinical form with their symptoms. After logging in , patients can track their status in real-time by viewing their assigned triage bracelet (based on the Manchester protocol), see the estimated wait time , check their consultation history , and receive notifications about their care status

EmergencySTS Backend (Developing)

he Backend is the secure web portal where Administrators manage staff user accounts and system configurations. Nurses use this system to review patient-submitted forms, register observations, and formally assign the triage priority bracelet. Doctors then access this prioritized list of patients to register diagnoses, issue prescriptions, and close their clinical episodes.

Mobile EmergencySTS (Developing)

This native Android app digitizes hospital triage with two main interfaces. The Patient side allows users to track their real-time triage status and estimated wait time. The Nurse (staff) side is a management tool used to access patient lists, create triage records, and assign priority levels based on the Manchester System.
